Mental Health Well-Being Assessment

Are you self-employed? This can influence your daily stress and support system.
Family history of mental health issues? For example, anxiety, depression or other conditions.
Currently taking treatment? Medication, therapy, counselling or any other support.
How long have you mostly stayed indoors? Think about your usual routine during the last few weeks.
Have you noticed changes in your habits? For example, changes in sleep, appetite or hobbies.
Do you have a mental health history? Such as previous diagnosis or treatment in the past.
Are you experiencing growing stress? Stress that feels like it is building up over time.
How often do you have mood swings? How often your mood changes a lot during a day or week.
Do you struggle to cope with things? For example, daily tasks feeling harder than usual.
Do you feel interested in your work/study? Loss of interest can sometimes be a warning sign.
Do you feel socially weak or isolated? For example, feeling alone or disconnected from others.
